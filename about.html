More About Me

I was born and raised in Kenya, where I received my elementary education at Hannington Academy in Oyugis before moving on to Darajambili Primary School in Kisii county in 2008. During my time at Darajambili primary school, I was elected school president in my 8th grade and went on to compete at the national student's government council, where I placed as the second runners up. My strong performance in my primary education exams earned me a spot at the highly selective Chavakali National School in 2016.

Throughout high school, I worked as a peer counsellor and eventually earned a promotion to the position of guidance and counseling captain in my 12th grade, overseeing the operations of 200 counsellors. I also consistently ranked as the top performing student in computer science, fueling my desire to pursue a career in the field. After completing my Kenya Certificate of Secondary Education exam in 2021, I applied to and was accepted to Cornell University during the early decision round, becoming the first boy from my hometown to earn a college education.
